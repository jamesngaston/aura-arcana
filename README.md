## 🔮 Key Features

### 📸 The Visionary Scanner
Bring your physical deck to life. Our proprietary vision engine allows you to scan your physical tarot spreads instantly. Aura Arcana identifies your cards and provides a deep, multi-layered analysis of your layout within seconds.

### 🤖 Celestial Technology Insights
Powered by state-of-the-art technology, our "Celestial Engine" acts as a personal oracle. It synthesizes the traditional meanings of the cards with the unique energy of your question, delivering readings that are shockingly accurate, deeply personal, and spiritually resonant.

### 🃏 Master the Spreads
Choose from a curated collection of spiritual layouts:
- **One Card draw**: Quick daily guidance.
- **4-Card Inline**: For a clear linear progression of energies.
- **The Compass**: Find your direction.
- **The Celtic Cross**: Deep, all-encompassing insights for life's biggest questions.

### 🌑 Premium Glassmorphic Design
Aura Arcana features a stunning, high-end dark-theme interface. With a sophisticated glassmorphic aesthetic and fluid micro-animations, every interaction feels as magical as the readings themselves.

### 🌍 Universal Wisdom
Tarot is a universal language, and so is Aura Arcana. With full support for **over 12 languages** (EN, ES, PT, FR, DE, IT, AR, HI, JA, KO, RU, ZH), you can connect with the divine in your native tongue.
