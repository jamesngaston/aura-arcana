## 1. Information Collection and Use

The Service Provider may require you to provide certain personally identifiable information to enhance your experience, including but not limited to your email address, full name, and user ID. 

### A. Automatically Collected Information
The Application may collect certain information automatically when you download and use it, including, but not limited to:
*   Your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address.
*   The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, and the time spent on those pages.
*   General usage analytics and diagnostic data.
*   The operating system used on your mobile device.

*Note: The Application does not gather precise location information from your mobile device.*

### B. Device Permissions (Camera & Photo Library)
To utilize the "Validate Cards" feature, the Application requires access to your device's Camera and/or Photo Library. 
*   **Purpose:** This access is used strictly to capture or upload a photograph of your physical tarot spread. 
*   **Processing:** The photograph is securely transmitted to our backend Artificial Intelligence (AI) services solely for the purpose of identifying the cards in the image. 
*   **Storage:** We do not permanently store, publicly share, or allow human review of these uploaded photographs.

## 2. Artificial Intelligence (AI) Processing

The Application utilizes advanced Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies, including Google's Gemini models, to provide core functionalities such as personalized tarot readings and image recognition.

*   **Processing of Prompts & Images:** The questions you type and the photographs of cards you upload are sent securely to our AI backend to generate your readings.
*   **Data Usage:** All AI processing is performed strictly to return your personalized content. The Service Provider does not use your submitted text prompts or images to train global, public AI models. 
*   If you have specific concerns regarding the AI processing of your data, please contact the Service Provider.
